Indian Railways (IR) is a statutory body...

It works under the ownership of the Ministry of Railways.
Government of India that operates India's national railway system.
      
Indian Railways Provide various functions to be Performed on it by the People.
    These Functions Are:-
        1-> Train Booking
        2-> Train Running Status
        3-> Seat Availability
        4-> Waiting List
        5-> Onboard Food Order
        6-> Train PNR Number
        etc.
